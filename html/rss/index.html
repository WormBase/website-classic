RSS News Feeds at WormBase

WormBase News feeds (using the RSS format) provide a convenient mechanism for staying up-to-date with new datasets, features, and system status. Browsers such as Apple's Safari and Mozilla's Firefox offer support for viewing and subscribing to RSS feeds.
WormBase-live
Subscribe to the WormBase-live RSS feed to learn about new features and datasets and to be notified of system status.
http://www.wormbase.org/rss/wormbase-live.rss
WormBase-dev
The WormBase-dev RSS feed describes new features and datasets currently under testing on the WormBase Development Server.
http://www.wormbase.org/rss/wormbase-dev.rss
WormMart
WormMart is the WormBase implementation of BioMart, a tool for retrieving datasets en masse. Subscribe to this feed to learn about new features and datasets at WormMart.
http://www.wormbase.org/rss/wormmart.rss
