2001 International Worm Art Show

The C. elegans community is fortunate to have generated many wonderful biological images (i.e. fluorescence micrographs) and artistic renditions of worms and embryos over the years. Those who have photographed or drawn such images are invited to display them during the poster sessions.  We had an excellent turnout last international meeting and we expect that there will be even more stiff competition this year.  Prizes will be awarded.
