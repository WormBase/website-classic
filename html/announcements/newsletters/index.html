WormBase Newsletter Archive

About the WormBase newsletter
The WormBase newsletter is published four times a year and distributed to members of the wormbase-announce mailing list. If you would like to automatically receive a copy of this newsletter, send an email to majordomo@wormbase.org. In the body of the e-mail (not the subject line) write "subscribe wormbase-announce". This is a very low-traffic mailing list.
