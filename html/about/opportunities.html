WormBase - Career Opportunities

WormBase offers a variety of positions involving software development, database design, curation, and user interface design.

Each WormBase site is seeking staff with interest in being at the center of the worldwide effort to understand this simple multicellular organism that serves as a major model for not only biomedical research but also all aspects of nematode research.

Researchers interested in a broad range of bioinformatics topics are urged to contact WormBase personnel since each site has training opportunities available at the graduate, undergraduate, and postdoctoral levels.
