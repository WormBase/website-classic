WormBase Logo Contest

WormBase announces its first ever contest to select a logo for the web site. We are looking for two kinds of logo: a masthead-style banner to display across the top of the web site, and a small square logo to use in correspondence, presentations and elsewhere.

Fabulous Prizes

The winner(s) will receive world-wide recognition, their names in lights, and a WormBase mug and/or tee-shirt.

Contest Rules

  1. Mail your submissions to wormbase@caltech.edu by July 15.
  2. For electronic submissions, please use GIF, JPEG or PNG format and one or more of the following sizes:
    Banners
    256x64, 320x64, 512x64, 256x32, 320x32, 512x32 pixels
    Square logos
    32x32, 64x64, 96x96 pixels
  3. For non-electronic submissions, sketch your ideas on a sheet of paper and mail to: 
          (Attn: WormBase Logo Contest)
      Wen Chen
      Caltech 156-29
      Pasadena, CA 91125
  4. Images should look good when reduced to 200 colors.
  5. Enter as many times as you like.
  6. WormBase staff and family members are welcome to enter, but will not get special treatment (we might be persuaded to give you a tee-shirt anyway).

Sneak Peak at Submitted Designs

As designs are submitted, you can view them at CalTech.
