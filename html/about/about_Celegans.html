Resources

C. elegans is a major model organism for biomedical research and for nematology research. Its 100 Mbp genome is complete, its developmental lineage is known to the cellular level, and the wiring diagram of its nervous system has been mapped out in detail.

Worm Papers Online
  • The Structure of the Nervous System of the Nematode Caenorhabditis elegans by J.G. White, E. Southgate, J.N. Thomson and S. Brenner 1986. Courtesy WormAtlas Site
  • Studies on the Development and Organisation of the Nervous System of Caenorhabditis elegans by R. M. Durbin 1987 HTML and PDF formats.
  • C. elegans II (CSHL Press, 1997) Courtesy NCBI
